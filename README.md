## 🧠 PROJECT OVERVIEW  

CyberTéranga AI is an **intelligent cybersecurity platform** that integrates:

- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence  
- 📡 IoT Security  
- 🔐 Cybersecurity  

➡️ To provide a **trusted digital environment for citizens**

---

## ⚠️ PROBLEM STATEMENT  

- 📰 Misinformation, fake news & deepfakes  
- 🔓 Vulnerable IoT devices  
- 🤖 Opaque AI systems (lack of explainability)  
- 🛑 No real protection for citizens  

---

## 💡 SOLUTION  

CyberTéranga AI is a **multi-layered platform** offering:

- 🕵️ AI-based misinformation detection  
- 📡 IoT device monitoring & anomaly detection  
- 🧾 Explainable AI (XAI) dashboards  
- 🔐 Secure & encrypted environment  
- 📊 Trust scores & real-time alerts
