## 🧠 Overview  

CyberTéranga AI is an **intelligent cybersecurity platform** that combines:

- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence  
- 📡 IoT Security  
- 🔐 Cybersecurity  

➡️ To create a **trusted digital environment for citizens**

---

## ⚠️ Problem  

- Fake news & deepfakes 📉  
- IoT devices vulnerable 🔓  
- AI systems not transparent 🤖  
- No real protection for users 🛑  

---

## 💡 Solution  

A **multi-layer platform** that provides:

- 🕵️ Fake content detection  
- 📡 IoT monitoring  
- 📊 AI trust scoring  
- 🧾 Explainable AI  
- 🔐 Secure environment
