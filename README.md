## 🧠 PROJECT OVERVIEW  

CyberTéranga AI is an **intelligent cybersecurity platform** integrating:  

- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence  
- 📡 IoT Security  
- 🔐 Cybersecurity  

➡️ For a **trusted digital environment for citizens**.

---

## ⚠️ PROBLEM STATEMENT  

- 📰 Fake news, misinformation & deepfakes  
- 🔓 IoT device vulnerabilities & hijacking  
- 🤖 Black-box AI systems, opaque decisions  
- 🛑 Lack of ethical safeguards & transparency  

---

## 💡 SOLUTION  

CyberTéranga AI provides:  

- 🕵️ **AI Detection Engine** (NLP, phishing, fake news, trust scoring)  
- 📡 **IoT Security Layer** (monitoring, anomaly detection, alerts)  
- 🧾 **Explainable AI Dashboards**  
- 🔐 **Cybersecurity Core** (encryption, authentication, access control)
